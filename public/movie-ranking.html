The Best Movies According to Sebastian

My top 3 movies of all-time

Spiderman

"with great power comes with great responsibility"

Spider-Man is a beloved superhero for many reasons. One of the most prominent reasons is that he is an everyman hero. He is a regular person gifted with extraordinary power and forced to accept the responsibility that comes with it through personal tragedy. His suffering creates a deeper sense of empathy for others, and he uses his powers to prevent strangers from having to endure the pain he has to live with forever.
Additionally, Spider-Man is a relatable character. He is a young guy whose silliness and awkwardness make him one of the most relatable superheroes to exist. He is also a hero who is not afraid to show his emotions and vulnerabilities, which makes him more human and endearing.
Finally, Spider-Man is a symbol of hope and resilience. He is a hero who never gives up, no matter how difficult the situation may be. He inspires people to be their best selves and to never lose hope, even in the darkest of times.

Fast & Furious

The Fast and Furious franchise has been a fan favorite for many years. One reason people love these movies is because they are action-packed and full of high-speed car chases. The franchise has also been praised for its diverse cast, which reflects the reality of America today.
Another reason people enjoy these movies is because they are centered around the theme of family. The characters in the movies are not just friends or acquaintances, but they are a tight-knit group of people who have each other's backs no matter what.

Iron Man

Iron Man is a beloved superhero for many reasons. One of the most prominent reasons is that he is a symbol of self-improvement and resilience. Tony Stark, the man behind the suit, is a flawed character who has been through a lot of personal struggles. He has been able to overcome his weaknesses and become a better person, which makes him an inspiring character.
Another reason people love Iron Man is because of his cool gadgets and technology. He is a genius inventor who has created some of the most advanced technology in the Marvel universe
